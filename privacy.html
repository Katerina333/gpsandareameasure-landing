Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Field Maps with Area Measure ("the app") is designed to work without an account and to keep your data on your device. This page explains what the app does and does not collect.

Location data

When you use GPS tracking, the app reads your device location to trace a boundary and calculate area, perimeter, and distance. Location is used only on your device to perform the measurement and is not transmitted to us.

Your measurements

Saved measurements are stored locally on your device. If you export a measurement (CSV or KML), the resulting file is shared by you, through your own choice of app.

What we do not collect

The core measuring features do not require an account and do not send your location or measurements to our servers.

Analytics & cookies

On this website we use Google Analytics to understand, in aggregate, how visitors find and use the site (for example which page referred them). Analytics cookies are only set after you accept them in the cookie banner — if you decline, no analytics cookies are stored. IP addresses are anonymized. This applies to the website only; the app itself does not use analytics for your measurements or location.

Android waitlist

If you join the Android waitlist on our website, we store the email address you submit solely to notify you when the Android version is available. We don't share it, and you can ask us to remove it at any time by emailing us.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email hello@gpsandareameasure.com.
