Privacy Policy

Last updated:

GPS & Area Measure ("the app") is designed to work without an account and to keep your data on your device. This page explains what the app does and does not collect.

Location data

When you use GPS tracking, the app reads your device location to trace a boundary and calculate area, perimeter, and distance. Location is used only on your device to perform the measurement and is not transmitted to us.

Your measurements

Saved measurements are stored locally on your device. If you export a measurement (CSV or KML), the resulting file is shared by you, through your own choice of app.

What we do not collect

The core measuring features do not require an account and do not send your location or measurements to our servers.

Questions about privacy? Email hello@gpsandareameasure.com.
