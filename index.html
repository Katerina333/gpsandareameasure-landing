Frequently asked questions

How accurate is the measurement?

The app uses geodesic calculations on the WGS-84 ellipsoid, so area and perimeter stay accurate even on large parcels. GPS accuracy depends on your device and sky view; tap-to-draw on satellite imagery is the most precise for small plots.

Can my whole team use it together?

Yes — that's what Workspaces are for. Invite teammates with a share code and give each person owner, editor, or view-only access. Everyone works from the same measurements, and changes sync automatically, even after working offline.

Does it work without internet?

GPS tracking and measurement work offline, and so do your workspaces — create, edit, and organize with no signal. Everything syncs to your team the moment you're back online. Map tiles for a brand-new area need a connection to load, but cached areas stay available in the field.

Which units are supported?

Acres, hectares, square meters, and square feet for area; meters, kilometers, feet, and miles for distance — switchable anytime.

Can I get my data out?

Yes. Export any measurement as CSV (for spreadsheets) or KML (for Google Earth and GIS software).

What devices are supported?

iPhone and iPad running iOS 17 or later, with an iPad-optimized layout.
